How publishing works

  1. Write or edit your post using the form below.
  2. Click Save as draft to save without publishing, or Publish to make it live.
  3. Click Download posts.json in the top bar to download your updated posts file.
  4. Go to your GitHub repository and navigate to posts/posts.json.
  5. Click the pencil icon to edit, then paste in the new file content and commit the change.
  6. Netlify will automatically redeploy your site within about 30 seconds.
